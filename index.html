How to Sell Gold in the UK

Selling gold is a simple way to turn unwanted jewellery, coins, or scrap gold into cash. The amount you receive depends on weight, purity, and the current gold market price.

In the UK, gold is commonly sold in 9ct, 14ct, 18ct, and 22ct. Higher purity gold contains more gold content and usually results in a higher payout.

What Affects Gold Value?

  • Weight in grams
  • Gold purity
  • Current gold price
  • Buyer margins and fees

Tips Before Selling Gold

  • Check the latest market rate
  • Separate gold by purity
  • Compare offers from different buyers
  • Choose a trusted UK gold buyer
